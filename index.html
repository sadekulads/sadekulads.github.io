About Me

Turning Ad Spend into Real Results

I'm Md. Sadekul Islam, a Google Ads specialist focused on lead generation and local service businesses — not e-commerce. My work is about getting the right people to call, book, or fill out a form — and then making absolutely sure that every one of those actions is tracked accurately.

Over the past few years, I've built expertise not just in running ads but in the full data infrastructure behind them. I implement cloud tracking solutions including Google Data Management (GDM), Google Forms Optimization (GFO), Google Ads conversion tracking, and Facebook/Meta Ads measurement — all wired together with server-side tagging to give clients the most accurate picture of their ROI possible.

I work closely with clients in local services (plumbers, cleaners, contractors, medical offices) and cultural institutions like museums, helping them compete smartly with precise targeting and reliable data.
